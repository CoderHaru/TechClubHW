Leading winners of some of the most prestigious football awards

List of footballers with most Ballon d'Or awards

The Ballon d'Or (French pronunciation: ​[balɔ̃ dɔʁ]; "Golden Ball") is an annual football award presented by French news magazine France Football that is one of the oldest and generally regarded as the most prestigious individual award for football players. The leading Ballon d'Or winners are as follows:

Lionel Messi Six
Cristiano Ronaldo Five
Johan Cruyff Three

List of footballers with most Golden Boot awards

The European Golden Shoe, also known as European Golden Boot, is an award that is presented each season to the leading goalscorer in league matches from the top division of every European national league. The trophy is a sculpture of a football boot. The leading Golden Boot winners are as follows:

Footballer Image No. of awards
Lionel Messi Six
Cristiano Ronaldo Four
Eusebio Two
Thierry Henry Two
Luis Suarez Two

Source of the above data


List of footballers with most IFFHS Playmaker awards

The IFFHS World's Best Playmaker is a footballing award which, since 2006, is given annually to the best playmaker of the year, as chosen by the International Federation of Football History & Statistics (IFFHS). The leading IFFS Playmaker of the year winners are as follows:

Footballer Image No. of awards
Lionel Messi Four
Xavi Hernandez Four
Andres Iniesta Two
Kaka One
Kevin De Bruyne Two
